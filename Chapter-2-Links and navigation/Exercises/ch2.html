The Mandalorian

The Mandalorian is a space Western television series set in the Star Wars universe, created by Jon Favreau. The show follows the journey of Din Djarin, a lone bounty hunter, and his quest to protect a mysterious child known as "The Child" or "Baby Yoda."

Key Information

Genre: Space Western, Action-adventure

Created by: Jon Favreau

Original Network: Disney+

First Episode: November 12, 2019

Seasons: 2 (as of now)

Official Website

Cast

  • Pedro Pascal as The Mandalorian / Din Djarin
  • Carl Weathers as Greef Karga
  • Gina Carano as Cara Dune
  • Giancarlo Esposito as Moff Gideon
  • Emily Swallow as The Armorer
  • Taika Waititi as the voice of IG-11

Characters

Din Djarin / The Mandalorian: A skilled bounty hunter with a mysterious past, tasked with protecting The Child.

The Child / Baby Yoda: A mysterious and powerful infant of the same species as Yoda, sought after by various factions.

Greef Karga: A leader of the bounty hunters' guild who becomes an ally to the Mandalorian.

Cara Dune: A former Rebel shock trooper who assists the Mandalorian on several occasions.

Moff Gideon: A former officer of the Galactic Empire who is determined to capture The Child for his own purposes.

Reviews

The Mandalorian has received critical acclaim for its storytelling, visual effects, and performances, particularly that of Pedro Pascal. The character of The Child has become a cultural phenomenon, earning the show a dedicated fanbase. Critics have praised the series for its ability to capture the essence of the original Star Wars trilogy while introducing new and engaging characters.
